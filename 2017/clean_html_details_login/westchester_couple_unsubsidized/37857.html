Design Backed by over 80 years of experience, Empire proudly serves members in its New York service area. Our HMO plans are designed to help you get healthy and stay healthy, and to get the care you need if you get hurt or sick. They include $0 in-network preventive care services for children and adults, prescription drug coverage with low co-pay generics, health and wellness programs like gym membership reimbursement as well as discounts on health related products and online tools to help you manage your plan. In addition, our provider network gives access to leading doctors and hospitals throughout our New York service area. The network includes primary care physicians who are committed to transforming their practices to a patient-centered model of care. Our HMO plans typically require a referral for specialist services except OB/GYN.
